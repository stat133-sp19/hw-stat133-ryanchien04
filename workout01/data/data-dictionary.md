team_name: The name of the team the player played for that game\
game_date: The date the game was played on\
season: The season (year) the game was played\
period: What period the shot was taken\
minutes_remaining: Minutes remaining in the period when the shot was taken\
seconds_remaining: Seconds remaining in the period when the shot was taken\
shot_made_flag: y if the shot was made, n if the shot was missed\
action_type: The type of shot that was attempted (i.e jump shot or dunk shot)\
shot_type: The value of the shot that was taken (2 point shot or 3 point shot)\
shot_distance: Distance the shot taken was from the basket\
x: The x coordinate of where the shot was taken\
y: The y coordinate of where the shot was taken\
